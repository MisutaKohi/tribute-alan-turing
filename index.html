Dr. Alan Turing

A founding father of Artificial Intelligence

His Life and Times:

  • 1912: Turing was born in Maida Vale, London
  • 1931-34: Studied at King's College in Cambridge, Top of his Class
  • 1939: Developed the idea of sequential analysis
  • 1940: Created the 'Bombe' used to decrypt Engima-enciphered messages
  • 1942: Traveled to US to work on their version of the 'Bombe'
  • 1946: Presented his paper on a strored program computer setup
  • 1947: Worked on the design of ACE, the Automatic Computing Engine
  • 1950: Developed the Turing Test to compare human and machine intelligence
