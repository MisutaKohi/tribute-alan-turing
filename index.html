Dr. Alan Turing

A founding father of Artificial Intelligence

His Life and Times:

  • 1912: Turing was born in Maida Vale, London
  • 1931-34: Studied at King's College in Cambridge, Honors Student and Top of Class
  • 1939: Develops the idea of sequential analysis
  • 1940: Creates the 'Bombe' used to decrypt Engima-enciphered messages
  • 1942: Traveled to US to work with US Naval Cryptanalysts on their version of the 'Bombe'
  • 1946: Presented his paper on a strored program computer setup
  • 1947: Worked on the design of ACE, the Automatic Computing Engine in London
  • 1950: Developed the Turing Test to compare human and machine intelligence
